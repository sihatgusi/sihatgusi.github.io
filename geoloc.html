Instruction

You can do this by standing in front of the entrance of the clinic and follow the instructions below:

Part 1:

  • 1. Enable Location Services/GPS on your devices.
  • 2. Make sure you allow the devices to assess your Location Services/GPS in Setting. This could take up to 1 minute.
  • 3. Open Web Browser eithers Internet Explorer, Firefox, Google Chrome, or Safari.


    • --- NEXT STEP ----


    Part 2:

  • 1. Stay at the same location
  • 2. Go to https://myketapang.github.io/geolocator/
  • 3. Wait until the Accuracy Score reaches less than 20 points.
  • 4. Click Send Email to complete the task.
  • 5. Fill-in the Email Subject as "Clinic Name" and finally click Send.

    • We will share the result of the mapping at the end of this project with you.
